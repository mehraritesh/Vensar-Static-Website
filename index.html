A note from the Managing Director on Vensar's growth, values and long-term direction.

Managing Director's Note

K. Jairam

Managing Director, Vensar Constructions

We are 25 years and still counting. We are glad to have reached a new milestone in our business. Even through challenging times, including the impact of the COVID period, we remained confident that India's infrastructure-led growth would create resilience, opportunity and large-scale employment.

Enhanced public investment, new developments and infrastructure expansion continue to create the right environment for progress. At Vensar, the true drivers of that progress are our people, and our continued focus has been on protecting their wellbeing while sustaining strong delivery standards across projects.

Our strategic imperative is to move beyond supplying items and instead provide complete solutions to end users. We will continue to serve clients with quality work, dependable service and uncompromising integrity. Honesty, collaboration, competency, creativity and respect have defined Vensar from the beginning, and our vision remains to deliver a customer experience second to none.

K. Jairam is an engineering graduate whose career journey spans global exposure, business analysis and large-scale automotive leadership before his full-time move into infrastructure and construction. After gaining international experience in the US and working in business analysis for Wells Fargo, he returned to India and helped expand the family's automotive group across multiple leading brands.

Today, Mr. Jairam operates more than 100 car showrooms and workshops across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Since joining Vensar Constructions in 2017, he has accelerated the company's entrepreneurial growth in infrastructure and construction, earning recognition through awards such as the Brand Slam Leadership Award and the Concrete Leadership Award.
