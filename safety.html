Safety & EHS

Clear controls for every active site.

EHS at Vensar is managed through simple routines: risk review, induction, work permits, inspection, training and corrective action. The goal is practical site control that protects people and keeps work moving responsibly.

Safety Framework

Focused controls for people, plant, machinery, permits, emergency response and continuous improvement.

Prepared people. Controlled work.

Every project needs the same fundamentals: induction, visible supervision, permit discipline, equipment checks and fast corrective action where risk is found.
